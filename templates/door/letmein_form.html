Let Me In!

The doors to Cameron Hall (the building that Unigames resides in) become locked outside of University Hours.
A student card is required to access Cameron Hall during this time.

This form allows you to send a message to the Unigames clubroom, requesting someone to come and let you in.

Please don't do this unless you are physically waiting outside an entrance to Cameron Hall.

Please note:

  • All usages of this system will be logged.
  • Abuse of this system (ie. requesting access when you aren't actually there as a joke) can result in disabling your access to this system.
  • Submitting this form does not guarantee that access will be granted.
  • Please stay at the entrance you've requested access to for at least 2 minutes before leaving.
