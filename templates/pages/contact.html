Contact Us

If you want to keep in touch with the comings and goings of the club, or contact the committee, we have numerous ways to do so!

Discord

Our Discord server is the fastest way to start chatting about your favourite traditional games with other Unigames members.
All of our events and major announcements are also posted there. It's the best way to keep in the loop!
Join our Discord Server!

Facebook

Our Facebook group is also available for discussion, recruiting players, and chatting.
All events and major announcements are also publicised though this group.
Join our Facebook Group!

Instagram

Our Instagram page serves as a way for members to stay up to date with the club's events. We also may post wrap up posts and various photos from our events, if you want to see what happens at our various events!
All events are also publicised though this page.
Follow our Instagram!

Email us

To contact the Unigames committee with any questions or comments, please email: uwaunigames@gmail.com (no spam please.)
